Description

Le Tour De Paris takes you on a whimsical photographic journey, captured on black and white film, as it playfully documents the Gilet Jaunes manifestations in the City of Lights. With a touch of humor, this captivating series unveils a tapestry of moments, exposing the raw emotions, resilience, and the absurdity of police violence, offering a satirical social commentary that leaves you both thoughtful and entertained.
